◈ OVERVIEW
Alien Legends is a browser-based battle RPG built within the Alien Worlds universe. Players recruit and develop Fighters — each with a unique race, class, and stat profile — and send them into dungeons, arenas, and tavern competitions to earn rewards and reputation.

The core game loop: travel to a Tavern to recruit new Fighters (costs Energy + Coins), then send a team of 5 into a Dungeon to battle enemies, earn XP, and collect Coins and Gems. As Fighters level up toward Level 10 they unlock greater power — at which point Ascension resets their level in exchange for permanent ability upgrades. Eventually, Arenas open PvP play between players' teams.

Owning Alien Worlds Arms and Crew NFTs grants battle modifier bonuses. The strength of each bonus scales with the card's rarity and shine tier — a single arms card and single crew card may be applied per battle. See the ALE STATS page for the full modifier table.

Each Fighter and ability belongs to one of six Elements: Air · Fire · Gem · Metal · Nature · Neutral. A planetwide Weather system rotates element buffs (e.g. +35% Gem damage), so checking the current weather before choosing a dungeon team can provide a meaningful edge.

Fighters grow through experience, levelling up to a cap of Level 10, after which they may undergo Ascension — a prestige mechanic that resets level but unlocks powerful new abilities and raises the power ceiling significantly.
◎ CURRENCIES & RESOURCES
🔋 ENERGY
The main action resource — used for everything. Enter a dungeon (10 Energy), hire a fighter at a tavern (100 Energy), and travel between planets to reach buildings on other worlds. Regenerates over time.
🟡 COINS
In-game currency earned from dungeon runs and selling levelled fighters. Used to reveal fighters at taverns, pay wages, and level up fighters. Spendable in-world.
💎 GEMS
Premium currency. Can be used to purchase Coins, boost buildings, and accelerate progression. Earned from top-tier dungeon pools and special events.

◉ STATS

Every Fighter has a set of core stats that determine performance in battle. Stats grow as the Fighter levels up and are further modified by Race bonuses and equipped AW NFTs. Below is a plain-English explanation of what each stat does.

❤️ HEALTH
The total amount of damage a Fighter can absorb before being defeated. High Health fighters withstand punishment; low Health fighters fall quickly under focused fire. Tanks are built around maximising this alongside Taunt. Higher is better.
💥 DAMAGE
The base amount of damage dealt per attack. Modified by the target's elemental resistance for the attacker's element. Some abilities scale off Damage or add bonus flat damage on top of it. Higher is better.
🛡 TAUNT
How strongly a Fighter attracts enemy targeting. Enemies preferentially attack the highest-Taunt Fighter in range. Taunt decreases each time the fighter is attacked, so tanks gradually become less magnetic as a fight wears on. Some abilities bypass Taunt entirely. Higher is better.
⚡ WINDUP (lower is better)
Determines attack order — who moves first at the start of a round. The Fighter with the lowest Windup acts first. A low Windup value means your Fighter strikes earlier, getting off damage and abilities before the enemy can respond. Think of it as initiative or speed.
🌀 COOLDOWN (lower is better)
The interval between a Fighter's attacks — how long they wait before acting again after each action. A lower Cooldown means faster re-activation and more frequent turns. High Cooldown (large number) means the Fighter waits longer between attacks. Think of it as attack speed in reverse: the number is the delay, not the rate.
🔮 ELEMENTAL RESISTANCES
Every Fighter has six separate resistance values — one for each element: Fire · Nature · Air · Gem · Metal · Neutral. A higher resistance value for a given element reduces the incoming damage from attacks of that type. For example, a high Fire resistance makes Fire-element attacks deal less damage to that Fighter. Some abilities can reduce an enemy's resistance, or ignore resistances entirely (resistance bypass). Higher is better for all six.
⬛ BUILDINGS

Buildings are the core infrastructure of your Alien Legends base. Each building type unlocks or improves different aspects of fighter management and progression.

Buildings are constructed by landowners on their Alien Worlds land plots. You don't build them yourself — you visit them. Two types exist at launch. Location matters: buildings on high-traffic tiles near portals attract more visitors, which increases footfall and shared reward payouts for the landowner.
⬡ TAVERN
RECRUITMENTPER PLANET
The primary source of new Fighters. Each Tavern is registered to a specific land plot on a specific planet. You travel to the Tavern coordinates, Reveal up to 3 fighter cards (costs Coins), then Hire one for 100 Energy.

Taverns support filter criteria that can reduce the hire discount: schema, rarity, shine, element, type, race, and stat thresholds. Match the criteria and your hire costs less. One recruit is permitted per Tavern per day — if you Reveal and walk away, a refresh may sometimes be needed before the next visit.
REVEAL COSTCoins
HIRE COST100 Energy
LIMIT1 hire / tavern / day

◌ DUNGEONS

Dungeons are the primary PvE content in Alien Legends. Send your Fighters in to defeat waves of enemies and earn experience, items, and rewards. Difficulty scales with dungeon tier.

⚔ HOW TO RUN A DUNGEON
MECHANICS
1. Travel to the dungeon's planet/coordinates (costs Energy if off-planet).
2. Select your team of 5 fighters. Consider current Weather and enemy element type.
3. Enter — costs 10 Energy. Battle resolves automatically based on fighter stats.
4. Collect rewards from the dual reward pool (player pool + building owner share).
📈 DIFFICULTY TIERS & XP
PROGRESSION
Dungeons scale across multiple difficulty tiers. Each step up in difficulty awards +5 XP per run and a more valuable reward pool. Running the highest tier your team can reliably clear is the most efficient path to levelling fighters. Attempting a tier above your team's power will result in a loss and reduced rewards.
ENTRY COST10 Energy (all tiers)
XP SCALING+5 XP per tier step
🌦 WEATHER SYSTEM
STRATEGY
A planetwide Weather system rotates element buffs continuously. For example, "Gem Weather" grants all Gem-element attacks a significant damage bonus (up to +35%). Always check the current weather before committing your team — swapping in fighters whose element matches the current weather can dramatically improve your run outcome.
ELEMENTSAir · Fire · Gem · Metal · Nature
WEATHER BUFFUp to +35% dmg
💰 REWARDS
LOOT
Each dungeon run draws from a dual reward pool: the player earns Coins and potentially Gems, while a share also flows to the dungeon building's owner. Higher difficulty tiers and more player traffic through a dungeon increase the overall pool. Running regularly on a high-traffic dungeon maximises both your payout and the building owner's income.
PLAYER REWARDSCoins · Gems (higher tiers)
BUILDING SHAREYes — passive for landowner
⬡ TAVERNS

Taverns serve as social hubs and secondary activity centres in Alien Legends. Fighters can rest here to recover, take on special quests, or compete in smaller-scale contests.

Taverns are where you find and recruit new fighters. Each one is tied to a specific land plot and planet — you must travel there to use it. Fighter availability differs between taverns, so visiting multiple can help you find the fighter type you need.
🗺 STEP 1 — TRAVEL
Navigate to the Tavern's planet and coordinates. If it's on another planet, this costs Energy to travel. The Tavern is owned by a landowner — high-traffic taverns near portals will have more visitors and may cycle fighters more frequently.
🃏 STEP 2 — REVEAL
Pay Coins to reveal up to 3 fighter cards. Each revealed card shows the fighter's class, race, element, rarity, and stats. You're not committed yet — you can look before you decide whether to hire.
COSTCoins
CARDS SHOWNUp to 3
✅ STEP 3 — HIRE
Select one of the revealed fighters and pay 100 Energy to hire them. Only one hire is permitted per Tavern per day. If you walk away without hiring, you may need to refresh the page before the Tavern resets for your next visit.
HIRE COST100 Energy
LIMIT1 per tavern / day
🎯 FILTER CRITERIA
Taverns can have filter criteria set by their owner that provide a hire discount when matched. Criteria include:

Schema · Rarity · Shine · Element · Type · Race · Stat thresholds

If your selected fighter meets the active filter, the hire costs less. Check criteria before choosing which fighter to hire — sometimes a less ideal fighter that matches the filter is the better economic choice.

Arenas host PvP combat between players. Enter your Fighters to test builds against real opponents, climb the seasonal rankings, and earn exclusive rewards.

Arenas are the PvP layer of Alien Legends — players pit their teams against each other for rankings and seasonal rewards. Arenas are not yet available in Open Alpha; this section will be expanded at launch.

✦ ASCENSION

At Level 10, a Fighter can undergo Ascension — a prestige mechanic that resets their level but permanently unlocks new abilities and significantly raises their long-term power ceiling. Ascending is a major strategic decision: the fighter becomes temporarily weaker, but the unlocked abilities can be game-changing.
