What is Naron Codex?

Naron Codex is an independent fan-built database and toolkit for the Alien Worlds metaverse. It aggregates template data, live market pricing, mint/burn stats, and strategic references across the Arms, Crew, Tools, and Faces schemas.

Built by players, for players — with no affiliation to Dacoco GmbH, Alien Worlds, or WAX.io.

Disclaimer

All data provided — including pricing, stats, and multipliers — is for educational and fan purposes only and should not be considered financial advice.

Market data is fetched live from AtomicHub and related WAX APIs. Accuracy is not guaranteed. Always verify on-chain before making decisions.
